Home > About Us > Overview & Identity

OUR-UNIVERSE is a top A++ School running successfully over 26 years. It is involved in nurturing students with
professional skills for their all-round development.

Overview:

Eastern Institute for Integrated Learning in Management (EIILM), Kolkata, founded in 1995, was established with an aim
to develop human potential in this ever-changing world, because ‘change is the only constant.’ Its glorious journey made the
institution a highly accep ted name in all the business sectors in India. Corporate houses of both National and International
repute rely on EIILM-Kolkata for promising candidates.


Over the years, EIILM-Kolkata has acquired the reputation of an esteemed institution for knowledge creation.
Faculty members conduct regular classes and workshops to acquaint the students with sound business theories and
their practical applications. The institute has been successful in nurturing students’ and making them corporate ready.

EIILM-Kolkata believes in the principle of 360° development of students by helping them to become
self-reliant in the corporate world. Regular training sessions help them acquire lucrative
positions in some of the best companies including a few of the Fortune 500s.
This has led EIILM -Kolkata to the path of recognition with numerous awards of excellence. To list a few:
1st in Top Eminent B-Schools of Super Excellence in India: by CSR GHRDC B-School Survey of India, 2020 “Best Placement among B-Schools” Education Excellence Award presented by ZEE 24 Ghanta, 2020 Featured in “Dun & Bradstreet” Leading Business Schools of India, 2019

EIILM-Kolkata has multiple collaborations with international universities. The Institute signed MOU with Mingdao University (Taiwan), Rajamangala University of Technology, Krungthep (Thailand) and Pathumthani University (Thailand). These collaborations facilitate student and faculty exchange programs and promote Academia-Industry interaction.

Our-Mission:

EIILM-Kolkata aims at developing human potential from the corporate, industrial, entrepreneurial, economic, social, intellectual, and cultural aspects. This will be achieved through state-of-the-art teaching, inculcating the ancient ethos of learning and educational facilities at par with the international standards. The mission of the Institute is also to encourage creative and innovative academic endeavors for management education, research, consultancy, and create socially responsible citizens.




Our-vision:

To be a renowned management institution with academic excellence that develops compassionate and committed corporate leaders with entrepreneurial attitude, socio-cultural, ethical, and environmental sensitivity.



Teachers always feel happy if their students experience happiness. Teachers consider the well-being of students as their well-being. Teachers consider happiness and well-being of students as their happiness and well-being.
